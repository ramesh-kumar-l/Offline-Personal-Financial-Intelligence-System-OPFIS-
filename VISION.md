# Offline Personal Financial Intelligence System (OPFIS)

## Vision Document

Version: 1.0

## Vision Statement

**Build the world's most trusted, offline-first, AI-native personal
financial intelligence platform that empowers every individual to
understand, preserve, and grow their financial life while maintaining
complete ownership of their data.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Executive Summary

OPFIS (Offline Personal Financial Intelligence System) is a new category
of software.

It is **not** an expense tracker, budgeting application, accounting
package, or portfolio manager.

OPFIS is a **Personal Financial Intelligence Platform** that combines:

-   Offline-first architecture
-   Local AI
-   Financial memory
-   Personal knowledge graph
-   Encrypted local storage
-   Explainable intelligence
-   Complete user ownership

Its purpose is to become the permanent financial memory of an individual
while ensuring that their financial history never has to leave their
device.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Mission

Everyone deserves intelligent financial software that respects privacy.

Our mission is to prove that world-class financial intelligence can
exist without surveillance, mandatory cloud services, advertising, or
vendor lock-in.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. The Problem

Modern financial lives are fragmented across:

-   Banks
-   Credit cards
-   Mutual funds
-   Stock brokers
-   Retirement accounts
-   Tax systems
-   Insurance providers
-   Digital wallets
-   PDF statements
-   Email
-   Spreadsheets

People have data everywhere but understanding nowhere.

-   Investments
-   Loans
-   Tax filings
-   Properties
-   Insurance
-   Goals
-   Major purchases
-   Financial milestones
-   Financial mistakes

The application becomes an explainable financial companion.

# 5. Core Thesis

The future of personal finance software is built around:

-   Understanding rather than recording.
-   Memory rather than transactions.
-   Local AI rather than cloud AI.
-   User ownership rather than vendor ownership.
-   Trust rather than data monetization.
